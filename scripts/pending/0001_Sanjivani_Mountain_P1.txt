# Episode 0001: Sanjivani Mountain (Part 1)
# Character: Hanuman
# Visual Theme: Mountain Night
# Moral: Devotion and creative problem solving

[INTRO - 30s]
Narrator:
Welcome to Tiny Tales of Bharat! Today, we follow the mighty Hanuman on a brave mission to save his dear friend, Lakshmana.

[SCENE 1 — THE CHALLENGE — 2m]
Narrator:
In the middle of the great battle, Lakshmana has fallen unconscious. Only one magical herb can save him — the Sanjivani, found on a distant mountain.

Rama:
“Hanuman… you are our hope.”

Hanuman:
“I will bring the Sanjivani, my Lord!”

(Visual: Moonlit battlefield, Rama kneeling beside Lakshmana, Hanuman preparing to leap.)

[SCENE 2 — THE MOUNTAIN — 2m]
Narrator:
After a long journey, Hanuman reaches the mountain. But there’s a problem — many herbs glow in the moonlight, and he cannot tell which one is the real Sanjivani.

Hanuman:
“If I cannot find the herb… I will take the whole mountain!”

(Visual: Hanuman lifting the glowing mountain under the starry night sky.)

[OUTRO — 1m]
Narrator:
And that is how Hanuman teaches us that devotion gives us strength, and creative thinking helps us solve even the biggest problems.

Narrator:
See you in the next tale — where the journey continues!
